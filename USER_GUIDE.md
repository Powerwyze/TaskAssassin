# TaskAssassin - Complete Feature Guide

## 🎯 What is TaskAssassin?

**TaskAssassin** is a gamified task management app that transforms your everyday chores and goals into exciting **missions**. Using cutting-edge AI technology, the app verifies task completion through photo evidence and provides personalized feedback in the voice of your chosen **Handler** (AI coach personality).

Think of it as: **Habitica meets Duolingo meets a spy thriller** – you're an agent completing missions, earning stars, maintaining streaks, and leveling up!

---

## 🌟 Core Purpose

**Stop procrastinating. Start assassinating tasks.**

TaskAssassin solves the age-old problem of task avoidance by making productivity:
- **Fun** - Gamified with stars, levels, and achievements
- **Accountable** - AI verifies you actually did the work
- **Social** - Compete and collaborate with friends
- **Personalized** - Choose an AI personality that motivates YOU

Whether you want drill sergeant intensity or supportive encouragement, TaskAssassin adapts to your style.

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. **AI-Powered Mission Verification** 🤖

- **Take a "Before" Photo** - Capture your messy room, cluttered desk, or starting state
- **Complete Your Mission** - Do the actual work
- **Take an "After" Photo** - Show your completed work
- **AI Analyzes Both Photos** - Google Gemini AI compares them and decides if you succeeded
- **Get Personalized Feedback** - Your chosen Handler gives you a unique debrief in their personality style

**Example:**
```
Mission: Clean your room
Before: Messy bed, clothes on floor, desk chaos
After: Made bed, organized desk, clean floor
AI Result: ⭐⭐⭐ 3 STARS! "Outstanding work, Agent. The enemy never saw this level of order coming."
```

### 3. **Gamification System** 🎮

- Complete missions to earn ⭐ (1-5 stars based on quality)

- Unlock achievements and badges
- Compete on leaderboards

#### **Streaks** 🔥

- See your longest streak record

#### **Achievements** 🏆

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Choose the RIGHT Handler** - Your motivation style matters. Drill Sergeant might demotivate if you need kindness!

2. **Start Small** - Don't create 10 missions on day 1. Build the habit with 1-2 daily tasks.

3. **Be Honest with Photos** - The AI is smart. Don't try to game it – you're only cheating yourself!

4. **Use Recurring Missions** - For habits like "make bed" or "exercise," set to weekly and build consistency.

5. **Leverage Social** - Assign missions to friends. Having someone else hold you accountable is powerful.

6. **Protect Your Streak** - The psychological power of "don't break the chain" is REAL. One task a day minimum!

7. **Experiment with Handlers** - Try different personalities! You might discover THE NOIR makes cleaning hilarious.

8. **Set Realistic Deadlines** - Give yourself actual time. Unrealistic deadlines = stress = failure.

9. **Chat with Your Handler** - Use the chat feature for motivation and suggestions. It's powered by AI!

10. **Celebrate Small Wins** - Every star matters. Every level up matters. Progress > perfection.
